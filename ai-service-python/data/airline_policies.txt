Refund Policy:
Tickets are fully refundable within 24 hours of booking.
After 24 hours, cancellation charges apply.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Baggage Policy:
Passengers are allowed one cabin bag up to 7kg.
Cabin bag dimensions must not exceed 55cm x 35cm x 25cm.
Checked baggage up to 15kg is allowed for economy class.
Business class passengers get 25kg checked baggage allowance.
Excess baggage charges are ₹500 per kg beyond the limit.

Rescheduling Policy:
Flights can be rescheduled up to 6 hours before departure.
Rescheduling fees may apply: ₹500 for domestic, ₹1500 for international.
Only one free reschedule is allowed within 24 hours of booking.
Changes must be made through the website or mobile app.

Cancellation Policy:
Cancel within 24 hours for full refund.
25-72 hours before departure: 25% cancellation fee.
72 hours to 7 days before departure: 50% cancellation fee.
Less than 7 days: No refund, only tax recovery possible.

Flight Delay and Cancellation Compensation:
For delays over 4 hours, passengers receive meal vouchers.
For delays over 6 hours, complimentary hotel accommodation if overnight.
If the airline cancels the flight, full refund or alternate flight is offered.
Force majeure (weather, strikes) may exempt compensation.

No-Show Policy:
Passengers who do not board without prior cancellation forfeit the ticket.
No refund or reschedule is allowed for no-shows.
Contact support at least 2 hours before departure to cancel and retain partial value.

Infant and Child Policy:
Infants under 2 years travel free on lap (one per adult).
Children 2-12 years get 50% fare discount.
Child seats can be requested at booking for a nominal fee.

Special Assistance:
Wheelchair assistance is free — request 48 hours before departure.
Unaccompanied minor service available for 5-12 years at extra cost.
Medical equipment and mobility aids carried free of charge.
